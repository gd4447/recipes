1. Trim & Prep ribs
	remove membrane
	coat with binder (mustard or oil)
	marinate if there is time
2. Set Up Grill 
	Start Smoker (use smoke box for gas grill)
	Set to stable 250 F
3. Cook bone side down for 2 hours
	refill smoke fuel as necessary
4. Cook bone side up for 1 hour
	refill smoke fuel as necessary
5. Take ribs off the grill and wrap in aluminum foil
	apply more seasoning or marinade if necessary
6. Cook wrapped for 1-2 hours until desired tenderness is reached
7. Remove from wrap. Coat with sauce (or final rub) and finish to internal temperature of ~190F
